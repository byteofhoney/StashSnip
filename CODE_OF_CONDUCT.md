# ☕︎ Code of Conduct

StashSnip is a small open source project and the goal is simple: be respectful and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and respectful in issues, PRs and discussions.
- Give constructive feedback. Critique code, not people.
- Be patient with beginners and first time contributors.
- Accept that not every idea will be merged and that's okay.

## Not Okay

- Harassment, insults or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory jokes or language.
- Spamming issues/PRs or derailing discussions off topic.
- Sharing anyone's private information without consent.

## Enforcement

If someone is being disrespectful or violating this, flag it by opening an issue or messaging the maintainer directly. Depending on severity, this may result in a warning, having comments/PRs removed or being blocked from the repo.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces issues, PRs, discussions and commit messages.

---

Basically: don't be a cynic and let's keep this a good place to learn and build together.
